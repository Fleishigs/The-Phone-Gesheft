Frequently Asked Questions

What makes these phones different?

Our phones are browser-free, meaning they don't have web browsers or access to social media. They're designed for essential communication—calls, texts, and select useful apps—without the distractions.

Can I still use WhatsApp or other messaging apps?

Yes! Our phones support essential messaging and communication apps. We'll help you choose the right phone based on which specific apps you need.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, return the phone in its original condition for a full refund.

Do you offer tech support?

Absolutely! Our team is here to help you get set up and answer any questions. Contact us by phone or email anytime.

How long does shipping take?

We ship within 1-2 business days, and most orders arrive within 3-5 business days via standard shipping.

Are these phones unlocked?

Most of our phones are unlocked and work with major carriers. Check the individual product page for specific carrier compatibility.

Can these phones be used for children?

Yes! Many parents choose our phones for their children because they provide essential communication without internet temptations.
