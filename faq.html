Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our phones

What makes these phones different?

Our phones are completely browser-free. You get all the essential features like calls, texts, GPS, and approved apps - without the internet browser that leads to endless scrolling and distractions.

Can I still use WhatsApp or other messaging apps?

Yes! Many approved messaging and communication apps work perfectly on our phones. We can help you set up the specific apps you need while keeping the browser disabled.

How does shipping work?

We offer free shipping on all orders over $100. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your phone, return it within 14 days for a full refund, no questions asked.

Can I customize which apps are installed?

Absolutely! We offer full customization services. Let us know which apps you need, and we'll configure your phone exactly to your specifications before shipping.

Do these phones work with my carrier?

Our phones are unlocked and work with all major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and their MVNOs. International compatibility varies by model - contact us for details.

How do I set up my phone?

Every phone comes pre-configured and ready to use. Simply insert your SIM card, turn it on, and you're good to go. We also provide detailed setup instructions and free technical support.

Can I re-enable the browser later?

The browser is permanently disabled at the system level. This ensures the phone stays distraction-free. If you need specific functionality, we can help find alternative solutions using approved apps.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Contact us and we'll answer any questions you have.
