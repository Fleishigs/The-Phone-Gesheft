Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about browser-free phones

What is a browser-free phone?

A browser-free phone is a smartphone that has had its web browser and other distracting apps removed or disabled. This allows you to use essential features like calls, texts, maps, and approved apps while avoiding the endless scrolling and time-wasting that comes with unrestricted internet access.

Why would I want a phone without a browser?

Many people find that unlimited internet access on their phone leads to decreased productivity, poor sleep, anxiety, and less meaningful real-world connections. A browser-free phone helps you stay connected to what matters while eliminating digital distractions. It's perfect for students, professionals, parents, and anyone seeking a healthier relationship with technology.

Can I still use important apps like maps, banking, or messaging?

Yes! Our phones come with essential apps that you choose. Common selections include GPS/maps, banking apps, ride-sharing, messaging apps like WhatsApp, music streaming, and other productivity tools. We work with you to customize which apps are available based on your needs.

What if I need to look something up online?

For occasional web access needs, we recommend keeping a computer or tablet available. Many of our customers find that this intentional separation helps them be more thoughtful about their internet usage and reduces impulsive browsing.

Will I still receive emails and notifications?

Yes, if you choose to keep email apps installed. You have complete control over which apps and notifications you want. Many customers choose to disable non-essential notifications to reduce distractions while keeping important ones active.

Can I install new apps later?

This depends on the configuration you choose. Some setups allow you to install approved apps, while others are more restrictive. We can help you find the right balance between flexibility and accountability based on your goals.
